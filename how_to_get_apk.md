# How to get your .APK File

Your web app is now **"APK Ready"**. It has its own custom icons and works offline!

Since you don't have Android Studio installed, you can use a free online converter to turn this project into an installable Android app.

## Step 1: Prepare your file
1.  Go to your project folder: `Desktop > Ashwin > Webdesign`.
2.  Select **ALL** the files inside it (index.html, style.css, etc.).
3.  Right-click -> `Send to` -> `Compressed (zipped) folder`.
4.  Name it `LuminaApp.zip` (or anything you like).

## Step 2: Convert to APK
1.  Open [WebIntoApp.com](https://www.webintoapp.com/) (It's free).
2.  Click **"Make App"**.
3.  **App Name**: `Lumina AI`
4.  **Company**: `Ashwin`
5.  **Icon**: Click "Set Icon" and upload the `icon-512.png` file from your project folder.
6.  **Zip File**: Upload the `LuminaApp.zip` you just made.
7.  Click **"Build"** (Using the "Free" plan is fine).

## Step 3: Install
1.  Wait about 1 minute for the build to finish.
2.  Download the `.apk` file.
3.  Send it to your phone (via WhatsApp, USB, or Drive).
4.  Open it on your phone and click **Install**. 
    *   *Note: You might need to allow "Install from unknown sources" since this is a custom app.*

## Alternative: Installation without APK
You can also just open your website in Chrome on Android:
1.  Go to your site URL.
2.  Tap the **3 dots** (Menu).
3.  Tap **"Install App"** or **"Add to Home Screen"**.
    *   *This works exactly like the APK but doesn't require a file download!*
